PING PONG BALL Game Instructonsi

  • Player Controls: Use your mouse to move the left paddle up and down.
  • AI Opponent: The right paddle is controlled by an AI and moves automatically.
  • Ball Mechanics: The ball bounces off paddles and walls, following realistic physics.
  • Scoring System:
    • The AI scores if the ball passes your paddle.
    • You score if the ball passes the AI’s paddle.
  • Winning Criteria: The first player to reach 5 points wins.
  • Game Over: A "Game Over" message will appear. Press F5 to restart the game.
  • Audio Feedback:
    • Paddle Hit Sound: When the ball strikes a paddle.
    • Score Sound: When a player scores a point.
    • Game Over Sound: Played when the game concludes.
  • Strategy Tip: Try to anticipate the ball’s trajectory and adjust your paddle position accordingly for better control.
