Snake Game Instructions

Objective: Control the snake to eat food and grow in length while avoiding collisions.

How to Play:

  • Use the Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) or WASD keys to move the snake.
  • Eat the food to increase your score and snake's length.
  • Avoid crashing into walls or yourself, or the game will end.

Scoring:

  • Each food item increases your score.
  • Your highest score (HiScore) is saved.

Tips to Win:

  • Plan your moves to avoid getting trapped.
  • Keep the snake moving in open spaces.
